POOF — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

POOF ("the app") is a usage tracking and reduction app. This privacy policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

The short version: We collect the minimum data needed to make the app work. We don't sell your data. We don't show ads. We don't share your tracking data with anyone.

What Data We Collect

Account Data

  • Authentication: Email address and/or anonymous authentication via Firebase Authentication. You can use the app with an anonymous account (no email required).
  • Profile: Display name (optional).

Tracking Data

  • Usage logs: Timestamps and counts of usage events you record. This data is the core of the app and is used to show you your patterns and progress.
  • Settings: Your preferences including daily targets, baseline estimates, and cost settings.
  • Onboarding responses: Baseline usage estimate and quit/reduction date (if set).

Device Data

  • Device identifiers: Anonymous device ID for crash reporting.
  • Platform: iOS or Android, OS version, app version. Used for debugging and compatibility.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the app's core features: Tracking, charts, progress visualization, and daily guidance.
  • To sync across devices: Your tracking data is stored in Google Firebase/Firestore so you can access it from any device signed into your account.
  • To improve the app: Aggregate, anonymized usage patterns (not individual tracking data) may be used to improve app features.

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell your data to anyone, ever.
  • We do not show advertisements.
  • We do not share your individual tracking data with third parties.
  • We do not use your data for marketing purposes.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, or other device features.

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored in Google Firebase/Firestore, which provides encryption at rest and in transit.
  • Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication.
  • We follow standard security practices for mobile applications.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your data is retained as long as your account exists.
  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile screen in the app.
  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Children's Privacy

POOF is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with data, contact us and we will delete it.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, use the account management features in the app or contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and/or on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
